Game Treatment

The Big Picture

You are a hare that hops around the screen. You must dodge owls while obtaining carrots to advance through levels.

Genre

Top-down Action

Platform

Web Application (Run in browser)

Story

Hares like yourself enjoy carrots. Go find some! But watch out for those owls!

Style

If possible, I would style this game should reflect old children’s fable books, but I cannot say I’m any sort of artist.

Gameplay and Mechanics

  1. The main mechanics of this game are as follows: The player clicks, the hare hops to that point. The hare cannot move any other way. You must time hops to avoid owls that attack anything in a radius.
  2. The controls: Left click to hop.
  3. The player is taught basic controls and objective at starting screen.
  4. The player will need to learn how to time their hopping best and how to get around owls.
